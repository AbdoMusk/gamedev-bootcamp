Team Management Exercises

Exercise 1: Communication & Role Clarity

Roles and Responsibilities:
Designer: Game visuals, UI. Communicates via chat, weekly meeting.
Developer: Code, bug fixes. Communicates via chat, daily standup.
Artist: Assets, animations. Communicates via chat, shared docs.
Producer: Schedule, tasks, support. Communicates via email, weekly summary.

Plan:
Daily standup (15 min, chat or call)
Weekly meeting (30 min, all roles)
Shared docs for updates
Email for summaries

--------

Exercise 2: Feedback & Iteration

Framework:
Observation → Impact → Suggestion

Example:
Observation: The jump animation feels slow.
Impact: Reduces game responsiveness.
Suggestion: Speed up animation by 20%.

Strategy:
Feedback given in meetings or chat
Team reviews, applies changes, documents results
Focus on actions, not people
Use neutral language, avoid blame

-------------

Exercise 3: Stress Management & Conflict Resolution

Stress Sources & Solutions:
1. Tight deadlines
   - Prioritize tasks
   - Take short breaks
2. Unclear roles
   - Clarify responsibilities
   - Use task board
3. Poor feedback
   - Set feedback rules
   - Schedule review sessions

Conflict Scenario:
Disagreement on feature design

Resolution:
Conflict type: Task
Steps:
1. Listen to all sides
2. List pros/cons
3. Vote or reach consensus
Leadership:
Stay neutral
Encourage open talk
Support final decision
