# What you changed
- Collectibles/Pickups now have a purpose, can be displayed and incremented.
- UI displays and is interactive with the real world.
- Restartable gameplay and game loop.
- Player can't double jump anymore.
- If the player goes down, he can die and the UI displays a message.
- A clear objective (Quest): reach the Blue Zone.
- Post processing.

# Why you changed it
to make the game more fun and give the player a reason to play. wanted to add more challenge and make the gameplay loop feel complete. also to fix some issues with jumping and player death.

# What the change improved
now the player has a goal and can see progress. the game feels more finished and less buggy. it's easier to understand what to do and when you win or lose. the UI helps a lot.
